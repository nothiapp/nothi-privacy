Delete Your Data Instantly (On-Device)

All notification data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it immediately:

1. Open your device Settings
2. Go to Apps → Nothi
3. Tap Storage → Clear Data
Or simply uninstall the app — all data is removed permanently

Request Deletion via Email

If you want us to confirm deletion or have questions, email us at:
nothiapp@gmail.com

We will respond within 7 days.

What Data Is Deleted

  • All notification history stored on your device
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status (local cache only — Play Store purchase record remains with Google)

What Is Not Deleted

  • Anonymized crash reports already sent to Firebase Crashlytics (retained up to 90 days by Google)
  • Anonymized analytics events already sent to Firebase Analytics (retained up to 14 months by Google)
  • Google Play purchase records (managed by Google, not us)
