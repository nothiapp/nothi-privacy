Privacy Policy

App: Nothi – Notification History
Package: com.notificationhistory.app
Last updated: May 9, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Nothi reads notification content (title, text, app name, timestamp) from your device to build a searchable history. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only (Room database)
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never shared with third parties

Sensitive content such as OTP codes is intentionally discarded and never stored.

2. Permissions Used

  • Notification Listener — required to read and store notification history. You must explicitly grant this in Android Settings.
  • Internet — used for ads (AdMob) and crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Billing — used for in-app purchases (monthly, yearly, lifetime premium).

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device advertising ID and usage data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage events (e.g., feature usage, session length). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports including device model, OS version, and stack traces. No notification content is included in crash reports.
  • Google Play Billing — handles premium subscription and purchase verification. Payment data is processed entirely by Google.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All notification data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Clearing app data via Android Settings → Apps → Nothi → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app

Free-tier users: notifications older than 30 days are automatically deleted.
Premium users: configurable retention (7 / 30 / 90 / 365 days or unlimited).

5. Children's Privacy

Nothi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

All notification data remains on your device and is protected by Android's application sandboxing. We do not have access to your notification data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
